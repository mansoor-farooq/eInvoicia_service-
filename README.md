# Digital eInvoicia

Modern digital invoicing web app  
100% integrated with FBR IRIS (Pakistan)  
You create invoices → system sends them to FBR automatically → you stay compliant

## What You Get

- Beautiful blue-white design that looks professional  
- Works perfectly on phone, tablet, laptop  
- Fast loading, smooth scrolling  
- Sticky navigation bar that follows you  
- Big hero section with strong headline  
- Animated cards and buttons that move when you scroll  
- Clean contact form with real-time validation  
- Mobile-friendly layout (Bootstrap 5)

## Purpose – Why This Exists

You run a shop, restaurant, or service business in Pakistan.  
FBR wants every invoice reported through IRIS.  
Doing it manually takes hours and causes mistakes.  

Digital eInvoicia solves this problem.  
You enter sale → click send → invoice goes to FBR in seconds.  
No more copy-paste, no more login to IRIS portal every time.  
Example: A shop sells 200 items in a month → owner saves 4–5 hours of work.

## Key Features

- Create professional invoices in under 30 seconds  
- Automatic real-time submission to FBR IRIS  
- Auto PSID generation for payments  
- Live sales tracking dashboard  
- Basic inventory management  
- Tax reports with one click  
- Mobile app access (future plan)  
- Three plans: small (200 invoices), unlimited, enterprise  
- 24/7 support on paid plans

## How the UI Looks & Works

- Top: Fixed navbar with logo + menu + blue "Get Started" button  
- Hero: Large background image + white big title "Digital eInvoicia" + stats (3+ businesses, 99.9% uptime, 24/7 support)  
- Services: Three cards – POS plan, Digital plan (most popular), Enterprise  
- FBR section: Dark background + white text + four benefit boxes with icons  
- Clients: Small logos of real businesses (Dairy Life, Youngs Bazar, Youngs Food)  
- Contact: White form card with name, email, phone, business type, message  
- Footer: Simple copyright + powered by line

Every section uses fade-in and slide animations so it feels alive.  
Colors: Main blue (#2d5be3), dark blue gradient, white background.  
No extra decoration – only useful things.

## Quick Start

1. Download or clone this repository  
2. Open index.html file in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge  
3. See the full website instantly (no installation needed)  
4. Click "Get Started" or "Start Free Trial" to test the contact form

## Real Usage Flow

- Visitor opens website  
- Reads hero section → impressed by FBR integration  
- Scrolls to services → sees three plans  
- Likes one plan → clicks contact button  
- Fills form (name, email, phone, business type, message)  
- Clicks Send → message arrives in your EmailJS inbox instantly
